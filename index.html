Pig Latin Rules

Translate words beginning with vowels

  1. Keep the word as normal. Do not change anything.
  2. Add "yay" at the end of the word.

Translate words beginning with a consonant or consonant cluster

  1. Move the consonant or consonant cluster from the start of the word to the end of the word.
  2. Add "ay" at the end of the word.

Letter Y rule

  1. If a word starts why the letter Y, it is treated as a consonant, and moved to the end of the word, following the usual rule.
  2. If the letter Y is the second letter in a two letter word, the first letter of the word is moved to the end of the word.
  3. If the letter Y is at the end of a consonant cluster, it is treated as a vowel, and does not move to the end of the word.
